#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Pyramid_of_the_Sun,29238|WD~Pyramid_of_the_Sun,29238]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Pyramid of the Sun is the largest building in Teotihuacan, 
> and one of the largest in Mesoamerica. 
> 
> It is believed to have been constructed about 200 AD. 
> 
> Found along the Avenue of the Dead, 
> in between the Pyramid of the Moon and the Ciudadela, 
> and in the shadow of the mountain Cerro Gordo, 
> the pyramid is part of a large complex in the heart of the city.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyramid%20of%20the%20Sun)
